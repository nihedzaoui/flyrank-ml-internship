# Case 1 — FlyRank AI Internship

## The Problem

Building machine learning projects is not enough. A recruiter needs to understand the problem, the decisions, and the outcome behind each project.

## What I Did and Decided

During the FlyRank AI Internship, I worked on improving how I present my AI work.

I organized my projects as case studies and focused on explaining:
- why the project existed,
- what decisions I made,
- what I learned.

I used AI as an interviewer to extract my real experiences while keeping my own voice.

## What Came of It

My portfolio changed from a collection of technical files into a clearer explanation of how I approach AI problems.

## What I Would Improve

I want to add more deployed applications and interactive demonstrations.
